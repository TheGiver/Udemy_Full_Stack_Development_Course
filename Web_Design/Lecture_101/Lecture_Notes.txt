Lecture Title: Introduction to Web Design
-----------------------------------------
Aim:
The aim of this module is to teach us the tricks and techniques that 
will help us develop attractive websites that look professional, authentic, 
valid and captivating. 

The reason why this aspect of design is vital is because most of the clients 
that will be dealing with the website do not really know about coding and everything
that goes into the building of the functionality of the website. All clients know is 
whether the website is easy to navigate and whether it looks nice or not. Therefore, we 
can see the vitality of this aspect of web development which emphasizes on the importance 
of knowing such tricks and techniques.
